How do I export my activity?

Grab a KML or TCX from your watch's website:

Garmin Connect — open an activity → ⚙ gear icon → Export to Google Earth (KML) or Export to TCX. connect.garmin.com

Strava — open an activity → ⋯ menu → Export GPX, then convert to KML/TCX (GPX import is on the roadmap).

Coros / Polar / Suunto — export the activity as TCX from the web dashboard.

TCX files add live heart rate, an effort-coloured route, and per-km splits.
